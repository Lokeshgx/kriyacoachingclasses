Privacy Policy

Kriya Coaching Classes is committed to protecting the privacy of students, parents, and visitors to our website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Student name, parent/guardian name
  • Contact details (phone number, email address)
  • Class/grade and academic details
  • Address (if required for communication)
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • IP address
  • Website usage data (pages visited, time spent)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for purposes including:

  • Providing and managing coaching services
  • Communicating with students and parents
  • Sharing academic updates, schedules, and announcements
  • Improving our website and services
  • Ensuring safety and security of our users

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share information only in the following cases:

  • With trusted staff and faculty for academic purposes
  • When required by law or legal processes
  • To protect the rights and safety of our institute, students, or others

3. Cookies and Tracking

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. However, no online platform is completely secure.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. Our systems and processes are regularly reviewed.

5. Third-Party Services

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (such as payment gateways or educational tools). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

Children’s Privacy

Since we provide services to students (including minors), we ensure that data collection is limited and used only for educational purposes. Parents/guardians are encouraged to monitor their child’s interaction with our services.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)

7. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with the revised effective date.
