Scholarship FAQs

Get answers to the most common questions about our scholarship process and eligibility.

Submit your application through the scholarship page, upload the required documents, and complete the eligibility form. Our team will review your profile and contact you with next steps.

Students with strong academic performance, leadership potential, or financial need are encouraged to apply. Each scholarship has its own eligibility requirements listed on the detail page.

Selection is based on academic merit, leadership qualities, financial need, and a personal statement. Each scholarship may include additional criteria, such as community service or subject excellence.

The review process typically takes 2-3 weeks. If additional information is needed, our team will contact you directly for clarifications or documentation.
