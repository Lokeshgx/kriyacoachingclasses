About Kriya Coaching class

Who We Are

Kriya Coaching Classes is a premier educational institution dedicated to providing personalized, supplementary education that bridges gaps in school learning.

Our Mission

At Kriya Coaching, We are committed to transform determined students into confident achievers—ready to excel in school, competitive exams, and life beyond the classroom with world-class academic mentorship, personalized growth plans, and a value-driven environment.

Our Vision

To nurture the next generation of thinkers, leaders, and achievers by building unshakable academic foundations and empowering each student to discover their highest potential with discipline, passion, and purpose

Our Ethos

At Kriya, we believe:

• Every student deserves mentors who are deeply committed to their growth.

• True education shapes not just brighter students, but stronger individuals.

• Discipline, resilience, and ethics are as important as marks and ranks.

• We stand with families who seek more than shortcuts—who seek real, lasting success.

• Success is built, not bought.
