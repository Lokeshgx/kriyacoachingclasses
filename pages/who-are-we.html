About Us

Who We Are & What We Do

Kriya Coaching Classes is a premier supplementary education centre based in Yelahanka, Bengaluru. Founded by Nitin Saxena — a researcher, academic mentor and educator with over 25+ years of teaching experience — Kriya was built with a singular purpose: to bridge the gap between what schools teach and what students genuinely need to thrive.

The name Kriya is derived from Sanskrit, meaning purposeful action. Every session we conduct, every student we work with, every plan we build — it is all purposeful. We do not fill pails; we light fires.

Our Story

Why & How Kriya Began

The Beginning — A Lifelong Teacher

Nitin Saxena began teaching when he was in Class 7 and never stopped. From home tutoring and school teaching to coaching institute sessions and NEET crash courses in PU colleges — teaching has always been both his passion and his purpose. Over 15+ years, he has taught over 3,000 students of varied age groups, mindsets, interests, and capabilities, personally witnessing their progress not just in academics but in life.

The Realisation — Education is Broken

Having worked across engineering, academic research (PhD, IISc Bengaluru), and corporate, Nitin saw first-hand that education in India had become a race to fill marks rather than build minds. Students were memorising, not understanding. They were scoring, but not growing. Most students leaving school were academically sound but personally underprepared for the real world. Something needed to change.

The Vision — Integrating Education with Life Skills

Kriya was founded on a bold idea: that a coaching centre could do more than prepare students for exams. It could prepare them for life. By integrating academic excellence with personal development, life skills, mental health support, and career clarity — Kriya set out to create a new kind of educational environment. One where students are known, valued, and nurtured as whole human beings.

“Education is not the filling of a pail, but the lightening of a fire.”
— William Butler Yeats

Kriya Today — A Growing Family

What started as a small, intimate tutoring initiative has grown into a full-spectrum educational centre serving 400+ students from 55+ schools across Bengaluru. With a team of expert, multidisciplinary faculty — researchers, trained counselors, language specialists, aptitude trainers and artists — Kriya continues to expand its reach while never losing its personal touch.

Our Difference

How Kriya Stands Apart

Most coaching centres focus on one thing. Kriya focuses on everything that matters.

Small Batches, Big Impact

We cap every batch at 8–15 students. Every student is known by name, tracked individually, and taught with their specific gaps and goals in mind — not as part of a crowd.

Holistic, Not Just Academic

We integrate mental health support, career counseling, life skills, and personal development alongside academics. No other coaching centre in our vicinity offers this complete ecosystem under one roof.

Personalised Learning Plans

No two students are alike. We diagnose each student’s strengths, gaps, and learning style before designing their roadmap. We teach the student, not just the syllabus.

Research-Backed Faculty

Our founder holds a PhD from IISc Bengaluru. Our faculty spans researchers, engineers, artists, counselors and language specialists — people who bring real-world depth, not just textbook knowledge.

Interdisciplinary & Integrative

We help students connect knowledge across subjects — because real-world problems are never neatly confined to one textbook chapter. We build thinkers, not rote learners.

Ethics & Empathy, Always

We believe academic excellence means nothing without character. Every interaction at Kriya is guided by empathy, respect, and a genuine commitment to each student’s long-term wellbeing.
