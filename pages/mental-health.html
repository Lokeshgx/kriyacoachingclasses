About This Service

Our mental health support programme offers one-on-one counseling, creative therapy sessions, and structured follow-up to help every learner thrive — academically and personally. We combine Adlerian therapy, art therapy, cognitive-behavioural techniques and mindfulness to create a truly holistic support experience tailored to each individual.

Who Can Benefit?

This programme is ideal for anyone experiencing emotional, psychological or academic challenges. You do not need a formal diagnosis to seek support.

  • Students facing exam or academic stress
  • Young people dealing with anxiety or depression
  • Learners with ADHD, ADD or learning differences
  • Individuals processing trauma or loss
  • LGBTQIA+ students seeking a safe space
  • Anyone feeling stuck, overwhelmed or burnt out
  • Parents wanting support for their child's wellbeing
  • Working professionals managing stress & burnout
